RECIPE

Microwave Christmas mug cake

Make a large cake using a 600ml mug or halve the mixture to make one small cake instead of two. Enjoy with brandy butter or custard!

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • ½ tbsp brandy
  • 25g raisins
  • 25g sultanas
  • 25g mixed peel
  • 4 glacé or dried cherries, chopped
  • 50g self-raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • 25g ground almonds
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon or mixed spice
  • 50g soft light brown sugar

Preparation

  • Melt the butter in a 300ml mug in the microwave for 20-30 seconds on high. Tip in the brandy, raisins, sultanas, mixed peel and glacé cherries and heat for a further 45 seconds.
  • Leave some of the remaining butter in the mug and use the rest to butter a second 300ml mug.
  • Add the remaining ingredients to the soaked fruit, mix to combine, then divide between the two mugs. Cook for 1 min 30 seconds until puffed up but not dry, then leave to stand for 2 mins.
